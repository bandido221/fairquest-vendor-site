What is FairQuest?

FairQuest is a mobile app and vendor platform designed to enhance the experience of Renaissance fair attendees and the incredible vendors who make these events magical. Our goal is to connect fairgoers with the people, shops, and attractions they love most—before, during, and after the fair.

How It Helps Vendors

  • Visibility: Feature your booth in our app so attendees can find you easily by name, type, or location.
  • Schedule: Let people know which fairs you're attending and on what dates.
  • Promotion: Share images, descriptions, and links to your website or social media.
  • Community: Join a growing network of artisans, performers, and merchants across the country.
  • Exclusive Bonuses: Early adopters who help us test the platform may qualify for highlighted listings and other future rewards!

Why Join?

FairQuest is more than just a listing—it's a community hub and discovery tool. Whether you're selling handcrafted jewelry, performing with fire, or running a food stall, we want to help people discover your magic.

Be part of something that helps Renaissance culture grow, connect with more visitors, and make every fair more successful—for everyone.
